Sir Thomas Sean Connery,

One of the greatest actors of all time (in my humble opinion).

Here is a bulleted list of some of the key points and acting roles during Sean's life:
  • 25 August 1930: Born in Edinburgh, Scotland
  • Late 1951: Started helping out backstage at the King's Theater
  • Early 1957: Sean hired an agent and got his first minor film role in
    Montgomery Tully's No Road Back
  • 1962: Married to his first wife, Diane Cilento
  • 1962: Sean starred in The Longest Day. A WWII movie that won 2 Oscar Awards
  • 1962: Sean had his major acting breakthrough as James Bond in the first Bond movie, "Dr. No"
  • 11 January 1963: His son, Jason Connery, was born
  • 1963: Sean starred in the James Bond movie "From Russia with Love"
  • 1964: Sean starred in the James Bond movie "Goldfinger"
  • 1965: Sean starred in the James Bond movie "Thunderball"
  • 1967: Sean starred in the James Bond movie "You Only Live Twice"
  • 1971: Sean starred in his last James Bond movie "Diamonds Are Forever"
  • 1973: Divorced with his first wife, Diane Cilento
  • 1975: Married his second wife, Micheline Roquebrune
  • 1987: Sean starred in the movie "The Untouchables", a gangster movie that won an Oscar
  • 1989: Sean starred in the movie "Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade"
    (personally my favorite of his work)
  • 2006: Sean retired from acting
  • 31 October 2020: Died in Lyford Cay, Bahamas, aged 90
